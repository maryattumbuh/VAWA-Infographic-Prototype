VAWA Housing Protections

What Is VAWA?

VAWA provides housing protections for survivors of:

  • Domestic violence
  • Dating violence
  • Sexual assault
  • Stalking

VAWA applies only to federally assisted housing programs

  • Does not apply to market-rate rental housing
  • Does apply if a voucher or other federal housing subsidy is involved

Protection from Eviction & Denial

  • Survivors cannot be evicted or denied housing because of violence committed against them.

Right to Confidentiality

  • Survivor information must be kept confidential unless the survivor provides written consent or disclosure is otherwise required by law.

Emergency Transfers

  • Survivors may request an emergency transfer to another unit or location for safety.
  • Reports are not required to request a transfer.

Right to Report

  • Survivors cannot be evicted, fined, or denied housingsolely for calling 911 or requesting emergency or law enforcement services.

Protection from Retaliation

  • It is unlawful for housing providers to retaliate against applicants or tenants for exercising VAWA housing rights.
